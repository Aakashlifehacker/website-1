Internet Explorer

  1. Make sure your bookmark bar is showing. If not, use "Control (Command on a Mac) + Shift + B"
  2. Drag the Button to your bookmarks bar.
  3. You're done!

If that doesn't work.

  1. 1) Right click on the Button.
  2. 2) Click "add to favourites"
  3. 3) Move it to your bookmarks bar
  4. 4) Click Okay!
  5. 5) You're done!
