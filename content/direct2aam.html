Direct2AAM: How to's Helping Author's Find AAMs

To make it easier for author to self-archive simply, quickly and correctly we’ve produced guides to turn the too often unsuccessful hunt for Author Accepted Manuscripts into a simple set of instructions that’ll always bring results. The guides, available for all major journals, provide simple to follow instructions for authors to obtain an Author Accepted Manuscript from their Journal Submission System, where the AAM is stored during the publishing process.
