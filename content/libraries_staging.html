Powerfully Simple Tools to  Deliver Articles and Fill Repositories

Libraries have led the charge to open up access to the scholarly literature. We help turn that leadership into leverage to reduce costs, to save staff time, to improve library services, and to open up even more content.
To do that we’ve built, in partnership with librarians, a suite of powerfully simply tools that help  deliver articles and  fill your repository. They’re easy to use and adopt free, legal, open-source, community-controlled tools to catalyse Open.
