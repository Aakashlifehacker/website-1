Open Access Button
We can open up a world of research

We all know the frustration of finding something you need but not being able to afford it. But what if it's already paid for? For publicly funded research, it could be!

The next time you can't access the research you need, use the Open Access Button to check if it is accessible, and if so to find a free version.

For research that isn't yet accessible, you can create a request - share your story and gain support from the community, and we'll follow up with authors and other sources.

If we can help you get access, your story can help show the real value of research. If you don't, your support can help change how it is published and shared in the future.
