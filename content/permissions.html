Automatic Permissions Checker

We’ve built an automated system, which is backed by open, community-editable, machine-readable data, to help librarians do permissions checking. The system provides all the information needed to help authors make a deposit. It covers 7,000 journals, 250 publishers, 80 university policies, several funders, and even authors’ negotiated contracts or waivers, to give a complete picture of what can be deposited. It works on one or even one hundred articles at a time, in seconds. Just copy and paste article titles, citations, or DOIs into our spreadsheet and you’ll get back all the article metadata and permissions information, such as embargos, licenses, completed deposit statements, and a summary to send to an author. If a policy is missing, needs an update, or is due a review, you can instantly contribute it, and we’ll archive the source and automatically monitor it for changes. This is a prototype for feedback from the community, but, soon, expect an API and new specialized tools for authors.
