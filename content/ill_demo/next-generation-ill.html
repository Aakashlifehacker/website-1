Next Generation ILL is nearly here!

We're working to build ILL forms that allow:

  • Instant delivery of OA and paid-for content
  • Embeding anywhere
  • Easy installation
  • Great on mobile
  • No data entry for patrons
all, Open Source, fully customisable and free. We're not quite ready for prime time, but here is a demo of what we're thinking.
