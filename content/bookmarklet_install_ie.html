Internet Explorer Install Instructions

  1. Make sure your bookmark bar is showing. If not, use "Control (Command on a Mac) + Shift + B"
  2. Drag the Button to your bookmarks bar.
  3. Click yes on the pop-up
  4. You're done! The Button is ready to click when you next need it.

If that doesn't work.

  1. Right click on the Button.
  2. Click "add to favourites"
  3. Move it to your bookmarks bar
  4. Click "Okay"
  5. You're done! The Button is ready to click when you next need it.
