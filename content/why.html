Why your story is important

If the Open Access Button helps you get access to the research you need, you can make progress with your work. If you don't get the access you need, you can create a request and we will continue to look for a copy. Once we find one, we'll email you with a link. In addtion, your story will be used to help change the publishing system so it doesn't happen again.

We know Open Access Button can only do so much, and isn't a perfect or permanent solution to this problem so your stories are key to help make the changes required to really solve this issue. We will be running data-driven campaigns and supporting grassroots advocates via our our Action Centre and you can play a major role by telling your story when you are denied access to research.
